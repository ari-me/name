KANIN

The logo of "Kanin" of a rice bowl with beef was inspired by a frequent recipe Arwen created. The spoon instead of classic chopsticks is one detail that makes "Kanin" unique from other recipe websites.

About Kanin



The website "Kanin" was created for a final web development project for the admin and client "Arwen Uy."

This website was created as an organization website to save and store the recipes that Arwen has made and wishes to make in the future. The website contains recipes of her top meals, kitchen tips that help one cook effectively and effeciently, and a essentials page where the most used ingredients and equipment Arwen uses are contained.

About Arwen


Arwen has been learning to cook multiple dishes for herself and her family since high school, looking through social medias and cooking websites every day with the thought "What should I cook today?"

The lists of recipes have piled up since and as such, it became hard to keep track of the done recipes and the want to do recipes as their all from different webpages. That's a part of the purpose of this website.

Arwen's personal cooking life is still full of learning and improvement, but it's always been fun to create new things for the ones she loves. Certainly, there is more progress to be done, but as she has said in all areas of her life, "improvement is what makes me live."
