Cooking Tips and Tricks

01

Clean Knives and Surfaces


For safe food preparation, don't cut raw meat, poultry or fish on the same surface or with the same knife without cleaning it thoroughly between uses. Wash your knife and cutting board with soap and hot water, or change the knife or board after each use.

02

Stop boiling water from overflowing


Place a wooden spoon lengthwise on top of the pot, and the water will not boil over. This works because the wood acts as a water repellant for the bubbles which stops it from rising, and the spoon being of a cooler temperature that when the bubbles meet the spoon, it condenses back into water.

03

Read the whole recipe before starting


It seems like common sense but is actually forgotten a lot. It's important to read the whole recipe from ingredients to the cooking process so that you know the basic structure and how it's made. It also helps if there are ingredients divided into portions that aren't shown in the ingredients section.
